PORRIDGE

Basic Ingredients:

  1. Yam
  2. 2 gallons of Vegetable oil
  3. a basket of Sweet corn
  4. Carrot
  5. Curry and Thyme
  6. Maggi and salt
  7. Hot dog
  8. Animal bile
    Directions
  1. Peal your yam properly
  2. cook it for 10minutes
  3. Add salt and basic ingredients
  4. Stir properly for 10minutes
  5. Cover it again and let it steam
